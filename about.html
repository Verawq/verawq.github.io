关于流星 About Meteor

概念区分 Concepts

· 流星体 Meteoroid

流星体是在太空中移动的固体物体，大小从尘埃颗粒到小行星不等，可以把它们想象成“太空岩石”。

Meteoroids are solid objects in space that range in size from dust grains to small asteroids. Think of them as “space rocks."

· 流星 Meteor

流星是流星体进入大气层并开始燃烧时，我们所看到的光迹。虽然被称为流“星”，但其实并非真的“星”。

Meteors are the streaks of light seen when meteoroids enter the atmosphere and start burning up. They are referred to as "shooting stars" but they are not really stars.

· 陨石 Meteorite

大部分流星体在落到地面之前便会在大气层中被消耗殆尽，少部分则会掉到地面上，称之为陨石。

When a meteoroid survives a trip through the atmosphere and hits the ground, it's called a meteorite.

流星分类 Types

按照周期性的不同，流星可以分为偶发流星和流星雨。

· 偶发流星 Sporadic Meteor

随机出现的流星被称作偶发流星。他们往往由岩石构成，通常来自小行星。如果两颗小行星发生碰撞，就会产生许多的小碎屑，在各自的轨道围绕太阳旋转。如果他们的轨道和地球的相交，那么便有可能形成流星。

Those random meteors are called sporadic meteors. They tend to be rocky in composition, and generally come from asteroids. If two asteroids smack into each other, the collision can eject little bits of material that then orbit the Sun on their own. If their orbit crosses the Earth, the you have a potential meteor.

· 流星雨 Meteor Shower

当流星体成群出现，流星雨就产生了。当一颗彗星围绕太阳运行，它上面的冰会升华成气体，并和尘埃、碎石等混合在一起。这些小颗粒会逐渐散落至轨道各处，形成一个围绕太阳的碎片带。当地球经过这一片碎屑云的时候，这些物质平行坠入大气就带来了流星雨。

Sometimes meteoroids travel in packs. When a comet orbits the Sun, the ice on it turns to gas, dislodging dust and gravel mixed in. this material leaves the comet and tends to stay more or less in the same orbit as the comet itself. Over time, that material gets scattered all along the orbit, creating a puffy ribbon of tiny pieces of space debris around the Sun. When the Earth plows through that cloud of debris, we get a meteor shower.

大部分流星雨都来自彗星，但有一个例外，那就是双子座流星雨，它来自小行星3200法厄同。这颗小行星离太阳很近，因此高温可能导致岩石汽化，使其与彗星很相似。

Most of meteor showers come from comets. But there is one exception, the annual Geminids shower, which comes from the asetroid 3200 Phaethon. This asetroid is on a orbit that takes it very close to the Sun. It is possible that the heat makes the rock vaporizes, making it act like a comet.

若从体积来看，大部分流星体的体积都很小，但有一些例外。

· 火流星 Bolide/Fireball

火流星是一种非常明亮的流星，比满月都要亮100倍，大约有柚子那么大。

A bolide, or fireball, is an extremely bright meteor, even a hundred times brighter than the full moon. They can be about the size of a grapefruit.
