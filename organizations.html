相关组织 Related Organizations

·International Meteor Organization (IMO)

国际流星组织（IMO）成立于1988年，是一家国际非营利性科学组织，成员遍布世界各地。该组织旨在鼓励、支持和协调流星观测，提高业余观测质量，向其他业余及专业人员传播观测结果，并对全球各地的观测进行分析。您可在该组织网站上发布流星、流星雨相关咨询，也可上报观测到的流星。

Founded in 1988, the International Meteor Organization (IMO) is an international scientific non-profit making organization with members all over the world. Its main objectives are to encourage, support and coordinate meteor observing, to improve the quality of amateur observations, to disseminate observations and results to other amateurs and professionals and to make global analyses of observations received world-wide. On the website, you can post your questions about meteors and meteor showers, and report meteors you observed.

·American Meteor Society (AMS)

美国流星协会（AMS）位于纽约州，是一家非营利性公司。公司旨在鼓励和促进对流星天文学感兴趣的业余和专业天文学者开展研究；观测、监测流星、流星火球、流星余迹及有关的流星现象，并收集信息、生成报告等。

The American Meteor Society, Ltd. is incorporated as a Not-For-Profit Corporation in the State of New York. The purposes are to encourage and promote research activities of both amateur and professional astronomers who are directly interested in meteoric astronomy; to observe, monitor, collect information, plot and report on meteors, meteoric fireballs, meteoric trains, and related meteoric phenomena.

·National Aeronautics and Space Administration (NASA)

美国国家航空航天局（NASA）负责美国的民用太空计划，也是全球太空探索的领先科研机构。探索NASA官网，可以了解包括流星在内的众多有趣好玩的天文知识。

The National Aeronautics and Space Administration is America's civil space program and the global leader in space exploration. Visit its website to learn interesting facts about astronomy, including meteors.
